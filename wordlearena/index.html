Wordle Arena: Word Battle — Support

Wordle Arena is a fast word-guessing battle royale for iPhone. Race AI rivals to crack the same five-letter word, climb six rank tiers, and collect trophies.

Contact

Questions, bug reports, or feedback? Email shalinikadavill@gmail.com. We aim to reply within 2 business days.

FAQ

How do I play?
Tap PLAY, pick your bot difficulty, and guess the hidden five-letter word in six tries. Green = right letter, right spot; yellow = right letter, wrong spot. Fastest correct solve wins the round.
Do I need an account?
No. Solo play against AI rivals works immediately, offline, with no sign-in.
Is the app free?
Yes — free, with no ads and no in-app purchases.
How do ranks work?
Winning matches earns rating that moves you through Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Champion tiers.
