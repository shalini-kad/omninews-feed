Wordle Arena: Word Battle — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Summary

Wordle Arena does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app has no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no server of its own.

Data we collect

None. Game progress (ranks, trophies, settings) is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or to third parties.

Game Center

If a future version offers online multiplayer through Apple's Game Center, matchmaking is handled entirely by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store any Game Center data.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data, it processes no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If our practices ever change, we will update this page and the effective date above before the change takes effect.
